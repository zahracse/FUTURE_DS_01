# FUTURE_DS_01
Business Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard using Tableau

## Project Overview
This project analyzes business sales data to understand revenue trends, identify top-selling products, and evaluate regional performance. The goal is to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights using data visualization.

## Tools Used
- Tableau Public
- CSV Dataset
- GitHub

## Dataset
The dataset contains e-commerce transaction data including:
- Invoice number
- Product description
- Quantity purchased
- Unit price
- Invoice date
- Customer ID
- Country

## Dashboard Features
The dashboard includes the following visualizations:

- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Top 10 Products by Revenue
- Revenue by Country
- Top Customers by Revenue
- KPI Metrics (Total Revenue and Total Orders)

## Key Insights
- The United Kingdom generates the highest revenue among all countries.
- A small number of products contribute significantly to total sales.
- Some customers generate a large share of the total revenue.
- Sales show noticeable changes across different months.

Dashboard Analysis
The interactive dashboard was created using Tableau Public to analyze business sales performance.
The dashboard consists of seven sheets that explore different aspects of the dataset and help derive business insights.

1. Monthly Revenue Trend
   
This sheet shows how revenue changes across different months. By visualizing revenue over time, we can identify trends and seasonal patterns in sales performance. The analysis helps businesses understand periods of high and low demand.

3. Top Products by Revenue
   
This sheet highlights the top 10 products generating the highest revenue. It helps identify which products contribute the most to the business’s overall income and allows businesses to focus on their most profitable items.

5. Revenue by Country
   
This visualization displays revenue distribution across different countries. The analysis shows that a large portion of sales comes from specific regions, particularly the United Kingdom, indicating a strong market presence there.

7. Top Customers by Revenue
   
This sheet identifies customers who generate the highest revenue. The analysis reveals that a small number of customers contribute significantly to total sales, which suggests the importance of maintaining strong relationships with high-value customers.

9. Total Revenue (KPI)
    
This sheet displays the total revenue generated from all transactions in the dataset. It provides a quick overview of the business’s overall sales performance.

11. Total Orders (KPI)
    
This sheet shows the total number of orders placed. It helps understand customer purchasing activity and overall transaction volume.

13. Dashboard Summary
    
All the sheets are combined into a single dashboard to provide a comprehensive view of sales performance. The dashboard allows users to quickly identify trends, top-performing products, key markets, and high-value customers.
